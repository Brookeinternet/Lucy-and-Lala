“IT’SUGAR: Cherry Twins Take Over the Candy Wonderland 🍬🍒”

The girls could barely contain their excitement as they walked deeper into the bright pink sugar haven. Their matching cherry overalls might’ve stolen the show—if not for the mountains of colorful treats around every corner.

Here are some of their favorite finds at IT’SUGAR:

  • 🎀 Sour Patch Kid Plushies – Soft, squishy, and just the right size for hugs (and pretend tea parties).
  • 🍱 Popin' Cookin' Kits – Tiny DIY candy sushi, donuts, and bento boxes! These kits are edible and fun to make with a grown-up.
  • 🍭 Giant Rainbow Lollipops – Nearly as big as their faces. One girl said, “I want the swirl one that looks like a unicorn tail!”
  • 🧁 Cupcake Bath Bombs – Not candy, but smelled just like frosting. (They asked if they could eat it.)
  • 🍫 Candy-Themed T-Shirts – “Sweet But Sassy” and “Snack Queen” were strong contenders for their next matching outfits.
  • 🐻 Gummy Bear Bulk Wall – So many colors. So many flavors. So many scoops into little paper bags.
  • 🧸 Candy Plush Adoption Corner – Where the girls picked out new sweet buddies and promised to "be their mommy forever.”

And yes, they left with smiles, new plush friends, a Popin’ Cookin’ kit, and pink sugar on their chins.
