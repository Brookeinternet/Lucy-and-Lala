🐸🍉 Lazy Morning Mood: Pajamas, Frogs & a Couch That Hugs Back 🛋️

Just one toddler, one couch, and zero rush. Some mornings aren't about getting dressed or checking boxes—they're about curling up in your comfiest jammies and letting the day unfold slowly, one blink at a time.

💤 Pajama Vibes:

  • ✨ Soft ivory cotton
  • ✨ Bright green frogs hopping around

This set is for the slowest kind of day. The kind where the sun sneaks through the blinds just enough☀️, the couch feels like a cloud☁️, and the only thing on the schedule is another snack (maybe…).
