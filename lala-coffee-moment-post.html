🍳 Homemade Waffles (Kid-Approved!) 🧇

Sometimes, a special morning treat is even better when it's homemade! Here's a simple, kid-friendly waffle recipe we love to make.

Ingredients:

  • 2 cups all-purpose flour
  • 2 tbsp sugar
  • 1 tbsp baking powder
  • ½ tsp salt
  • 1¾ cups milk
  • ⅓ cup melted butter (or vegetable oil)
  • 2 large eggs
  • 1 tsp vanilla extract

Optional Add-Ins:

  • Mini chocolate chips 🍫
  • Mashed banana 🍌
  • Cinnamon 🍂
  • Rainbow sprinkles 🌈 (just for fun!)

Instructions:

  • Preheat your waffle iron.
  • In a large bowl, whisk together the flour, sugar, baking powder, and salt.
  • In another bowl, beat together the milk, melted butter, eggs, and vanilla.
  • Pour wet ingredients into the dry and stir just until combined. Don’t overmix!
  • Lightly grease the waffle iron. Pour in the batter and cook until golden brown.
  • Serve warm with maple syrup, fruit, or a swirl of whipped cream.

Pro Tip for Toddlers:Cut into bite-sized squares and serve with a tiny fork for maximum joy and minimal syrup mess. 😉
